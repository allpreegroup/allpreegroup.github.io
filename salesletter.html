Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì What exactly is Allpree?

Allpree is a **Savings Club** that turns your everyday spending on groceries, fuel, and utilities into **structured savings**. You spend like normal, your savings accumulate automatically, and you access them at the end of each savings cycle.

‚ùì Do I need to invest or recruit people?

No. Allpree is not an investment program. You do not recruit anyone. You simply use your normal spending to **accumulate structured savings** over time.

‚ùì How do my savings accumulate?

When you purchase vouchers for in-store use, a portion of the extra amount you pay is allocated to your **Savings Balance**. Your balance grows automatically as you continue spending, and it is released at **month-end** according to the structured savings cycle.

‚ùì When can I access my savings?

Savings are released at the **end of each savings cycle** (typically month-end). This ensures your accumulated savings are stable and predictable.

‚ùì Do I earn anything extra from my savings?

Your savings grow based on the **voucher pricing structure** you use. The more consistent your spending and voucher purchases, the faster your structured savings accumulate. No interest or external investment is involved‚Äîjust predictable savings growth.

‚ùì Is there a fee to participate?

Yes, there is a **Platform Assurance Fee** to ensure your savings cycle runs smoothly and the system remains sustainable. Active members typically recover this cost quickly through their structured savings accumulation.

‚ùì What happens if I don‚Äôt spend every month?

Allpree works best for members who have regular, recurring spending. If you don‚Äôt spend, there is no savings accumulation for that period.
