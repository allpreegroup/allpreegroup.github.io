✅ What We Do

Allpree helps people fund everyday shopping through a structured savings approach built around prepaid value, digital vouchers, and local deal discovery.

At the center of the system is the Allpree Card, a smart prepaid shopping card that members load with flexible voucher amounts. These vouchers are not tied to specific items or stores. Instead, they activate a private savings balance when used at participating locations.

How it works:

  • Members load their Allpree Card with voucher values such as J$10,000 to J$20,000.
  • Each voucher contributes to a structured savings balance held within the club.
  • Making a qualifying in-store purchase activates the savings cycle.
  • Savings accumulate privately over a fixed 121-day club period.
  • At the end of the cycle, accumulated savings are released back to the member.
  • Different membership tiers unlock higher savings potential and access benefits.

What members get:

  • A simple prepaid shopping card built for everyday spending.
  • Structured savings that grow quietly in the background.
  • Access to local in-store deals through the app and website.
  • Clear visibility into their savings balance and cycle progress.

Allpree doesn’t change where people shop. It changes how they prepare, plan, and save while doing it.
