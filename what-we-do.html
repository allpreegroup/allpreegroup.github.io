✅ What We Do

Allpree transforms how people fund their shopping by combining digital vouchers, cashback rewards, and local brand discovery into one seamless experience.

Our platform is built around the Allpree Card, a smart digital gift card that users top up with shopping vouchers. These vouchers aren't tied to specific products or stores. Instead, they unlock high cashback rewards, which are activated when users shop at participating partner stores.

How it works:

  • Users top up their Allpree Card with voucher amounts like J$10,000 upto J$20,000.
  • Cashback is earned at the time of top-up, not on the item purchased.
  • A single store purchase activates the cashback, starting a 121-day payout countdown.
  • After 121 days, cashback is paid directly to the user’s bank account.
  • Depending on their membership tier, users can earn 30% to 49% cashback on voucher top-ups. This model lets us offer much higher returns than typical loyalty systems because the rewards are pre-funded and managed internally.

What users get:

  • A simple, smart shopping card that earns cash.
  • Access to local brand deals, refreshed frequently via app and website.
  • Clear cashback tracking with countdown timers.
  • Direct payout to their bank after the waiting period.

Allpree gives people a smarter way to shop, not by changing where they shop, but by changing how they fund it.
