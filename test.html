How the Cashback Loop Works

This system is built to be simple, sustainable, and safe. You don't need to know anything about finance or investing to benefit.

Spend as Usual

Buy gift cards through the app for things you already spend on – groceries, clothes, utilities, etc.

Earn Cashback

Get real cashback on every purchase. Your cashback enters a reserve and is held for 121 days.

Choose Your Boost

After 121 days, withdraw your cashback or leave it for 365 more days to unlock up to 100% of your original spend value.

The 121-day delay makes the system strong, allowing for cash flow management, fraud prevention, and bigger, sustainable rewards. You track your earnings daily, transparently.

Sustainability & Trust

Unlike other fast-reward schemes that crash, the Cashback Loop is built to last. Our unique reserve model and delayed payouts ensure stability and protect everyone.

The Reserve Model

A portion of every gift card purchase is earmarked for your cashback reserve, allowing time to manage risk, prevent fraud, and build liquidity.

Power of Holding

Choosing to hold your cashback longer (365 days) not only boosts your payout but also strengthens the entire system for all users.

Transparency & Control

No games, no hidden fees, no pressure to recruit. You see your cashback daily and have full control over when to withdraw.

Not a Scam, No Risk

You're not investing, just shopping differently. You're buying real gift cards for real merchants and earning real cashback. You can't lose capital.

Community Supported

When users hold cashback longer, they strengthen the reserve, reduce payout pressure, and help us offer even better promotions.

Flexibility to Withdraw

You can always choose to withdraw after 121 days with your guaranteed portion, demonstrating the system's flexibility.

Your Two Paths: Choose Your Journey

The Cashback Loop offers flexibility. You decide how deep you want to go and what works best for your financial goals.

You buy gift cards, earn cashback, and it's held for 121 days. After this period, it becomes eligible for payout at the next window. No strings attached.

Good for you if...

  • You need access to your funds sooner.
  • You want to test the system first.
  • You're just looking for a simple reward from regular spending.
⏳

Path 2: The Cashback Loop Route

After the 121-day period, you choose to leave your cashback in for 365 more days to unlock a boost of up to 100% of your original spend value.

Good for you if...

  • You want to turn everyday spending into a future payout.
  • You're not in a rush for small cash now, you want real money later.
  • You like the idea of your cashback growing passively.

You can switch between paths, or even do both! The power of the Cashback Loop is that it's flexible and entirely yours.
