Install Our App

STEP ONE

Add our app to your home screen:

  1. Tap the three dots (menu) in the top right corner of your browser.
  2. Select "Add to Home Screen."
  3. Tap "Add" and you're done!

✅ Next Step:
Go to your phone's home screen and look for AllPree (the app you just added).

Hold the app icon and drag it to the first page so it's easy to find later!
