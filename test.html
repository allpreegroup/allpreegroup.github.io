Unlock More: How the Cashback Loop Works

Ready to turn everyday spending into long-term wealth? Once you create your free account, you can activate the Cashback Loop.

Spend as Usual (with Account)

With your free account, buy gift cards through the app for things you already spend on.

Earn Account-Bound Cashback

Your earned cashback is securely tied to your free account and enters a reserve, held for 121 days.

Choose Your Boost (via Account)
