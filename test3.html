1. Browse Deals by Brand
Deals are grouped by brand, sorted by best discounts and expiration.
Look for highlighted Takeover or Favorite brands — these open automatically.
Badges like Pro, Premium, Starter, Exclusive indicate special deal types.
Use Show All Other Brands to explore every brand during takeover.
2. View Deal Details & Navigate
Tap any deal to open the Deal Popup with pricing, cashback, and expiration info.
Navigate deals with Next and Previous buttons.
Like deals to save favorites and Add to Cart to prepare for purchase.
3. Manage Your Favorites
Access your Liked Deals mode to quickly find saved deals.
Favorites are saved locally and remembered across sessions.
4. Shopping Cart & Lists
Add deals to your cart for easy checkout.
View cart items by brand with full price breakdowns including tax.
Clear items per brand or save shopping lists for later.
5. Store Locations & Directions
Find store locations for brands in the Location Popup.
Enable location access to get directions from your location to the store.
6. Tips & Tricks
Check Takeover Brands first for exclusive deals.
Deals shuffle each session for fresh offers.
Watch deal Timers to grab expiring deals.
Use Show All Other Brands to see all deals.
7. Troubleshooting
Refresh the page or check your connection if deals don’t load.
Allow location access to use maps and directions.
Clearing browser data removes saved favorites and cart.
