2. 🎯 Takeover Brands
  • 🎯Takeover Brands automatically open when you launch the app.
  • They highlight special or featured deals curated just for you.

  • ⏳Takeover deals often have limited-time offers, so watch the timers!
3. 🔍 View Deal Details
  • 🔍Click a deal to see price, cashback, and expiration.
  • 🔃Swipe up or down to move between deals.
  • ❤️Tap to like or Add to Cart to prepare purchases.
4. ❤️ Favorites
  • ❤️Tap the heart icon to save deals you like.
  • 🔍View all liked deals in Liked Deals mode.
  • 📁Favorites are stored locally and auto-loaded on return.
5. 🛒 Cart & Shopping List
  • 🛒Add deals to your cart to track purchases.
  • 💵See subtotals, tax, and grand totals by brand.
  • 📃Clear brand-specific items or save a list for later.
6. 📍 Store Locations
  • 📍Click Location to find nearby stores.
  • 📉Enable location access to get directions in Google Maps.
7. 🧪 Tips & Tricks
  • 🌟Takeover Brands often have the best offers.
  • 🔄Deals reshuffle every session — check back often.
  • ⏳Keep an eye on timers — some expire quickly.
  • 🔍Use Show All Other Brands to browse everything.
