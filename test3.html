1. Browse Deals by Brand
- Deals are grouped by brand and sorted by:
   • Best discounts
   • Soonest expiry times

- Featured brands include:
   • Takeover: Special spotlight brand
   • Favorite: Your preferred brand

- Badge labels like Pro, Premium, Starter, Exclusive show deal types.
- Use Show All Other Brands to reveal more.
2. View Deal Details & Navigate
- Tap any deal to view:
   • Original price
   • Discounted price
   • Cashback and final cost

- Use Next / Previous to browse deals.
- Tap ❤️ to save a deal, or Add to Cart to plan a purchase.
3. Manage Your Favorites
- Tap ❤️ on any deal to like it.
- View all saved items in Liked Deals Mode.
- Likes are stored on your device and remembered across sessions.
4. Shopping Cart & Lists
- Add deals to your cart for easier planning.
- The cart groups items by brand and shows:
   • Subtotal
   • 15% tax
   • Final total

- You can clear a specific brand’s cart.
- Save shopping lists for later with date and brand name.
5. Store Locations & Directions
- Tap the Location button to view store options per brand.
- Allow location access to:
   • Find nearby stores
   • Get directions using Google Maps
6. Tips & Tricks
- Always check Takeover Brands first — they have featured deals.
- Deals shuffle every session, so you’ll see fresh offers each time.
- Pay attention to countdown timers — some deals expire soon.
- Tap Show All Other Brands to view the full list.
7. Troubleshooting
- If deals don’t load:
   • Refresh the page
   • Check your internet connection

- Enable location access for maps and directions.
- Clearing browser data removes your saved likes and cart.
