Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the cashback gift card system work?

Sign up to get a universal digital gift card accepted at all partner merchants. Buy gift card vouchers from us at a 25% markup. After 121 days, receive up to 49% cashback paid to your bank.

2. Where can I use the gift cards?

Gift cards are redeemable at local physical stores that participate in the gift card system managed by our partner. Any store listed in their network can accept the digital gift card we provide you. Our app shows deals and promotions from some of these local stores to encourage shopping. We track only the gift cards you buy from us and manage your cashback payouts accordingly.

3. Where does the cashback come from?

The cashback comes entirely from our company’s income. When you buy a gift card, the full value goes directly to our partner, who manages the gift card system. We use a portion of the markup (company income) to generate returns through our internal operations. That’s what powers the cashback you receive.

4. Do I need a membership?

No. But members pay J$8,400 per year to unlock up to 49% cashback on select gift card vouchers.

5. What are the cashback tiers?

Free members: 30% cashback
Paid members:
 - J$20,000 vouchers → 49% cashback
 - J$15,000 vouchers → 41% cashback
 - J$10,000 vouchers → 37% cashback
(All cashback paid 121 days after purchase.)

6. When do I get paid?

Exactly 121 days after your gift card purchase, your cashback is sent directly to your linked bank account.

7. Is this a financial product or investment?

No. This is not an investment. You are purchasing a retail gift card. Cashback is a loyalty reward funded by our company’s income, not from your payment.

8. What happens if I don’t use the gift card?

Gift card vouchers are valid at listed stores. To activate the cashback payout after 121 days, you must make at least one purchase using the gift card following your gift card voucher top-up.

9. Can I see my purchases and payouts?

Yes. Your app dashboard shows all gift cards you’ve bought, expected cashback, and payout schedule. To view your balance and in-store transactions, please check our partner’s gift card system.

10. Will stores pay to be listed in the app?

Eventually, yes. For now, listing deals is free for participating stores.

11. How are payouts sent?

Payouts are made via bank transfer to your linked Jamaican bank account. You’ll need to provide your account number and bank name in the app to receive funds.

12. Can I get paid in cash or mobile wallet instead?

For now, we only support payouts to Jamaican bank accounts. We may add support for wallets like Lynk or JN Pay later on.

13. Is this available outside Jamaica?

No. At this time, cashback rewards are only available to users in Jamaica with a local bank account.
