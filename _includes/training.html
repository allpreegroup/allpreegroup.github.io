how to join

Step 1: Download the GiftMe App

Then reach out to the member who recommended you here. They will provide you with the company's private WhatsApp number to express your interest in joining the company.

Once your account is approved, we will send you a digital EXPENSE GIFT CARD via email for you to save within the GiftMe App, This allows you to shop in-store at any of our 300+ partner locations islandwide.

Finally, we will send you a special code via WhatsApp. Use this code when sending payments to us via the Lynk app. It enables us to accurately send the cashback to the right recipients after 121 days
