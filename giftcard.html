How It All Works - Behind the Scenes

The two core systems that make our cashback rewards real, dependable, and built to last.

⚫ Cashback Fund

The Cashback Fund is an internal operating float, privately held and funded by a portion of Allpree’s revenue.

It is managed under strict internal guidelines to support company growth and maintain platform performance.

A majority of its surplus is allocated to strengthen the Cashback Reserve.

🟩 Cashback Reserve

The Cashback Reserve is a secure float used exclusively to pay eligible users who have completed their 121-day cashback period after a qualifying in-store or bill payment.

It is funded by voucher purchases and supported by allocations from the Cashback Fund.

Buying vouchers helps reinforce your own future rewards.

Allpree Earns Revenue In a Few Simple Ways

  • Voucher Markups: Each voucher added to your Allpree Gift Card includes a small markup. This helps fuel the cashback system while keeping things simple for users.
  • Cashback Membership Fees: Users can upgrade to unlock higher cashback rates. These membership fees directly support the platform and strengthen the Cashback Reserve.
  • Brand Membership Fees: Businesses pay to join Allpree and list their in-store deals. This keeps the platform filled with fresh offers.
  • Featured Deal Promotions: Brands pay to highlight their best deals in top spots. More visibility for them, more opportunities for users.
  • Affiliate Fees from Brands: When you redeem your Allpree Gift Card at a brand's store, they pay a fixed fee to Allpree, but only when you actually shop.

A portion of these revenues goes into the Cashback Fund, then the majority of its surplus is allocated to the Cashback Reserve.

The Fund Powers: Growth.
The Reserve Powers: Your Payouts.

Together, they create a closed-loop system that works without gimmicks, without dependency on future users, and without delay. Just long-term reliability.

You are not just earning cashback. You are part of a system designed for fairness, stability, and shared value.
