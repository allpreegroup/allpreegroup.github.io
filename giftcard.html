How the Allpree Gift Card Works

The Allpree Gift Card simplifies your shopping and rewards you. Here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

1. Create An Account

Install the Allpree app, then use the invitation code to create your account. enter the card information in the app to generate your Allpree Gift Card.

2. Buy Vouchers

Purchase gift card vouchers directly through the app to add funds to your digital Allpree Gift Card.

3. Pay At Merchant

Use your Allpree digital gift card to pay for goods and services at Giftme merchant across Jamaica.

4. Earn Cashback

After 121 days from your purchase, receive cashback directly into your bank account. The longer you hold, the more you earn!

This system allows for sustainable rewards and full transparency, with your earnings tracked daily in your Allpree account dashboard.
